# IPC: Offences Against the Human Body

Section 299 defines culpable homicide.
Section 300 explains when culpable homicide amounts to murder.
Section 302 prescribes punishment for murder: death or imprisonment for life, and fine.

Section 304 applies when culpable homicide does not amount to murder.
Section 304A covers causing death by rash or negligent act when there is no intention to cause death.

Section 307 criminalises attempt to murder.
Section 319 defines hurt, and Section 320 defines grievous hurt.
Section 323 punishes voluntarily causing hurt.
Section 325 punishes voluntarily causing grievous hurt.

Section 354 addresses assault or criminal force to a woman with intent to outrage modesty.
Section 376 addresses rape, including aggravated forms and punishment bands.
