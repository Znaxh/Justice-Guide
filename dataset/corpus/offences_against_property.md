# IPC: Offences Against Property

Section 378 defines theft as dishonest moving of movable property from another's possession without consent.
Section 379 provides punishment for theft.

Section 390 defines robbery in relation to theft and extortion using violence or fear of instant violence.
Section 392 provides punishment for robbery.

Section 405 defines criminal breach of trust as dishonest misappropriation or conversion of entrusted property.
Section 406 provides punishment for criminal breach of trust.

Section 415 defines cheating.
Section 420 punishes cheating and dishonestly inducing delivery of property, with imprisonment up to seven years and fine.

Section 463 defines forgery.
Section 468 deals with forgery for the purpose of cheating.
Section 471 addresses using a forged document as genuine.
